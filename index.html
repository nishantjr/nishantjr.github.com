About

I’m making this blog to document my escapades, teach myself writing, practice building web-sites, show off my doodles, try my hand at design and more importantly - to have fun.

I try to do as much is reasonable by hand - HTML, CSS, the drawings and images to let me appreciate the effort that goes into doing these things. I had started off trying to write my own static site generator, and got it working too, but jekyll is so much nicer, so I’m going with that now.

I’ve been writing about the various (mis-)adventures I’ve been having - nearly getting arrested for stealing my own bicycle, a rained-on attempt at climbing Mt. Fuji or bicycle rides.

So share and enjoy!
