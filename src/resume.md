As a developer, I'm a proponent of test-driven development and a firm
believer of continuous improvement -- every time you touch a piece of code,
you leave it better than it was before. It's important for me to have an
intimate knowledge of the tools I work with, and find the time to delve into the
workings and design decisions taken in the platforms, languages and runtimes
that I use. It's important to me to have both breadth of knowledge as well
as depth and I make it a point to learn at least a little about important
and popular technologies and tools even if I don't use them myself.
